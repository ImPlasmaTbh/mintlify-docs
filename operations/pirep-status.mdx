All PIREPs that have been filed are automatically processed. These may take a few minutes to be processed, depending on server load.

Leaving a PIREP comment either through vAMSYS or Pegasus will result in your PIREP being sent for manual review by staff.

### PIREP Status Definitions

| PIREP Status    | Description                                                                                                                                                                         | Action Needed                                                                                                                |
| --------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Accepted        | The PIREP has been manually reviewed and accepted by a staff member. You will be awarded points and hours for this flight.                                                          | None.                                                                                                                        |
| Complete        | The PIREP has been reviewed automatically, did not meet any of the autoreject conditions and was not sent for further review. You will be awarded points and hours for this flight. | None.                                                                                                                        |
| Rejected        | The PIREP has been manually reviewed by a staff member and rejected. You will be awarded any hours flown, but no points will be added.                                              | None.                                                                                                                        |
| Invalidated     | The PIREP has been manually reviewed by a staff member and invalidated. You will not be awarded any hours or poitns for this flight.                                                | None.                                                                                                                        |
| Awaiting Review | The PIREP has met one of the autoreject conditions and has been sent for further review by a staff member.                                                                          | None.                                                                                                                        |
| Reply Needed    | The PIREP has been reviewed by a staff member and requires further attention/information from you.                                                                                  | Reply to the PIREP and provide the required information. You cannot book another flight until you have replied to the PIREP. |
