## Welcome to Emirates Virtual

Emirates Virtual is a flight simulation based virtual airline dedicated to replicating real-life operations of Emirates Airline. This knowledgebase contains everything you need to know to start soaring the skies and flying better.

<Info>
  Emirates Virtual is a non-commercial hobbyist group and is in no way endorsed or affiliated with Emirates, its subsidiaries or any other airlines. All logos, images, and trademarks remain the property of their respective owners.
</Info>
